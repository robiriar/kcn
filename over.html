Overview

Khalsa College of Nursing was added as a milestone in the year 2006 with the aim to prepare well qualified nurses to improve nursing services. It is widely recognized as a center of excellence in the field of nursing education with excellence in job training facilities in North Zone. The institution has highly qualified and experienced Faculty. The college has well-equipped laboratories and Library for Nursing Students with all the modern Facilities. The college has well ventilated classrooms updated with modern technology. The college possesses good and comfortable accommodation for students with all the facilities like round the clock security, entertainment, boarding and mess. The College of Nursing came into existence in 2006 with the intake of 60 students of B.Sc. Nursing. Post-Basic B.Sc. Nursing and GNM Courses were started in 2009 with an intake of 30 & 40 seats respectively. M.Sc. Nursing was also added with an intake of 20 seats with four specialty areas in the year 2010. ANM is just started with intake of 30 students in 2012. Democratic atmosphere is maintained among the student and teachers. Student�s personal problems are looked into and necessary counseling and guidance is given by the teachers. The students are free to approach any faculty member for the purpose. Special emphasis is placed on the recreational facilities for the personal and social development of the students. Picnics, parties and variety of entertainments are organized by the student council of Khalsa College of Nursing to provide ample opportunities to the students to exhibit their talents. Medical services of the Dispensary attached to College/ Institute are available to the students. The devout religious minded founders of the college attached highest importance to the study of religion and the cultivation of moral values in students. The college provides regular religious instructions, but there is no compulsion of any kind for non-Sikh students. It is compulsory for all the students and staff members to participate in Nagar Kirtan on the eve of Guru Nanak�s Birthday. The College is duly affiliated with India Nursing Council, Punjab Nurses Registration Council and Baba Farid University Health Sciences, Faridkot. The Institution has come up by dint of hard work and sincere efforts of management staff, employees and also the cooperation of students.
