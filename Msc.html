Eligibility Criteria

The candidate must has passed B. Sc. Nursing/Post Basic Nursing securing minimum 55% marks and 1 year of teaching or clinical experience.
The candidate must be registered with Punjab Nurses Registration Council.
Students shall be medically fit.

Entrance/Selection

Selection of the candidates should be based on the merit of the entrance examination held by Baba Farid University

Duration

Duration of the course shall be 2 years.

Direct Admission

No Direct Admission
