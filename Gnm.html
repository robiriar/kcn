GNM Course

Total No. of Seats: 40

Eligibility Criteria

Minimum and Maximum age for admission shall be 17 and 35 years respectively.
Minimum education :
1. 10+2 class passed or its equivalent preferably with aggregate of 50% marks.
2. Those who have cleared 10+2 vocational ANM course (revised after 2001) from the school recognized by Indian Nursing Council.
Students shall be medically fit.

Entrance/Selection

The selection of candidate shall be made on merit, based on marks obtained by them in 10+2. The college has total 40 seats.

Duration

Duration of the course shall be 3 years.
