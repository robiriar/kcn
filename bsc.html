BSc Course

Total No. of Seats: 60

Eligibility Criteria

The candidate must has passed Senior Secondary or any other examination are is equivalent thereto.
The candidate must have completed General Nursing & Midwifery Course. She must be registered with respective state nursing council as approved by Indian Nursing Council.
Students shall be medically fit.

Entrance/Selection

TThe selection of candidate shall be made on merit, based on marks obtained by them in the qualifying examination i.e. GNM.

Duration

Duration of the course shall be 4 years.

Direct Admission

No Direct Admission
